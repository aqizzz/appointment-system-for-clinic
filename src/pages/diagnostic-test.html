Blood Test - Complete Blood Count (CBC)

The items included in a complete blood count (CBC) are

  • Phite blood cell count (WBC)
  • Red blood cell count (RBC)
  • Hemoglobin (Hgb or Hb)
  • Hematocrit (Hct)
  • Platelet counM
  • Mean corpuscular volume (MCV)
  • Mean corpuscular hemoglobin (MCH)
  • Mean corpuscular hemoglobin concentration (MCHC)
  • Red cell distribution width (RDP)
  • Differential white blood cell count, including percentages of neutrophils, lymphocytes, monocytes, eosinophils, and basophils.
